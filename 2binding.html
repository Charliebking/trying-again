Charlie King

Binding

This series shows a trans-masculine person putting on a binder. This is a task that a trans person does nearly every day and it is quite a chore to put on. It is a tight chest binding garment. I wanted to showcase the trans body as well as putting on a binder because this is rarely shown in art or media, and is even less commonly shown in a positive light.
